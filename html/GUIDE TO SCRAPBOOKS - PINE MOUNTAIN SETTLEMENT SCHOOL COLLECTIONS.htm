GUIDE TO SCRAPBOOKS

TAGS: Guide to Scrapbooks; scrapbooks; memorabilia; newspaper articles; magazine articles; photographs; letters; tributes

This guide to the multiple scrapbooks held in the Pine Mountain Settlement School archive is a rich source of regional history. The scrapbooks range from approximately the early 1800s until approximately the late 1980s. The first two scrapbooks were gathered by Pine Mountain School staff while the remaining scrapbooks are the efforts of individual donors who were associated with the School.
